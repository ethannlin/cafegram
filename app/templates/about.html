privacy

in order to access features within the spotify SDK, this application needs your permission to enable user scopes (such as 'playlist-modify-public' which allows us to create playlists and add tracks on your behalf). we do not store or distribute any of your information. permission can be revoked through this page.

note: due to limitations with the spotify SDK, this app will only work for spotify premium users.

cafégram

cafégram aims to offer users a relaxing and enjoyable environment, merging the joy of music with the coziness of a cafe setting. It serves as a digital retreat where users can unwind, discover new music, and experience a cafe-like ambiance from the comfort of their own devices. It provides an immersive experience by simulating a cafe environment virtually, allowing users to explore and listen to music from spotify's extensive library.

due to copyright restrictions, the music featured on cafélib is not currently available for public access. however, cafélib serves as a proof of concept, offering a glimpse into the potential of the web app.
